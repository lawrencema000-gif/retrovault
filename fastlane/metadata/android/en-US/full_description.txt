Pulsar is an open-source (GPLv3) emulator for portable and classic PlayStation-family systems,
with a built-in store of games that are legally free to distribute.

FEATURES
- Emulates PSP, PS1 and PS2 via well-established open-source cores.
- A curated store of homebrew, public-domain, freeware and permissively-licensed indie games —
  browse cover art, tap, download and play.
- Import your own games and console BIOS from your device (Storage Access Framework).
- Touch controls + Bluetooth gamepad support, save states and cloud save sync.
- Clean, fast, dark UI.

LEGAL
Pulsar does not host, bundle, or distribute copyrighted commercial games or console BIOS files.
Commercial titles and BIOS are supplied by you, from your own legally-obtained copies. The app is
open-source; complete corresponding source ships with every release.

Emulators are legal. Distributing copyrighted ROMs is not — so Pulsar's store contains only content
we have the right to distribute, and everything else is yours to import.
